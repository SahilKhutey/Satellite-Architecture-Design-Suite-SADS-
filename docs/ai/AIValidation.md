## 1. Safety Reviews

All AI copilot recommendations are audited against hard safety thresholds:
1. **Explainability**: Recommendations must provide standard literature references.
2. **Safety limits**: Advisor cannot recommend designs that violate basic conservation laws or exceed structural mass capacity constraints (e.g. wet mass $>180\text{ kg}$).
