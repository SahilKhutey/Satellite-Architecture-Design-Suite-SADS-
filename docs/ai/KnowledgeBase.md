## 1. Reference Material

The AI copilot references standard spacecraft design manuals:
* **Wertz SMAD (Space Mission Analysis and Design)**: Primary reference for EPS, orbits, payloads, and ADCS.
* **NASA-STD-5001**: Structural design criteria.
* **ECSS Standards**: European space standards for systems engineering.
