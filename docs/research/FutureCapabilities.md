## 2. Planned Capabilities
* **XR Collaborative Environment**: Allowing multi-user XR assembly design over WebSockets.
* **Autonomous Constellation Deconfliction**: Automated collision avoidance (COLA) maneuver planning.
* **Radiation Environment Modeling**: Shielding optimization against proton/electron fluence.
