# Technical References & Constants

## 1. Physical Constants
- Solar Constant at 1 AU ($G_0$): $1361 \text{ W/m}^2$
- Stefan-Boltzmann Constant ($\sigma$): $5.670374419 \times 10^{-8} \text{ W/m}^2\text{K}^4$
- Standard Gravitational Acceleration ($g_0$): $9.80665 \text{ m/s}^2$
- Speed of Light ($c$): $299792458 \text{ m/s}$
- Earth Radius ($R_E$): $6378.137 \text{ km}$
- Earth Gravitational Parameter ($\mu$): $3.986004418 \times 10^{5} \text{ km}^3/\text{s}^2$
