## 1. Overview
The SADS Research Roadmap lays out core areas of active R&D to improve the scientific precision and simulation fidelity of the digital engineering suite.

---

## 2. Research Areas
* **Multibody Attitude Dynamics**: Expanding ADCS estimation to include flexible panel solar vibrations.
* **Non-Keplerian Low-Thrust Trajectories**: Trajectory optimization algorithms using continuous low-thrust electric propulsion (e.g. Hall-effect thrusters).
* **Thermal Network Radiator Optimization**: Deep reinforcement learning models to size and route fluid radiator tubing.
